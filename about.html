About us

Great dentistry, no drama

We're a modern practice that believes looking after your teeth should be simple, affordable and genuinely pleasant.

Our story

Law & Order: Dental Unit opened its doors at Deltic House on Silverlink Business Park with a simple mission — to take the stress out of visiting the dentist.

We've grown into a friendly local practice serving families, professionals and students across Wallsend and the wider Tyne & Wear area. Whether you're here for a routine check-up or you've been putting off a visit for years, our team will make you feel welcome and at ease.

Everything we do is built around clear communication. We'll always explain what's going on, lay out your options honestly, and let you decide what's right for you.
